What is Sustainable Agriculture?

Sustainable agriculture is the practice of farming using principles that protect the environment, public health, and animal welfare. It integrates three main goals: environmental health, economic profitability, and social equity.

From crop rotation and organic fertilizers to agroforestry and precision irrigation, every technique aims to produce food without depleting natural resources or harming ecosystems.
